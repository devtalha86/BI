The performance and growth of your business are visually represented using attractive Statistical Charts so you can understand them with ease.

Integrating with Internal Database

To reflect real-time transactions and other services on the interactive reports and visualization, our system needs to be connected with your internal and external data sources, often a database. When a transaction or change is made on the database, it will reflect in the BI. The system would enable you to keep track of the performance of your agents/branches without making any extra effort.
The system is developed in a user-friendly way that users will be able to access all of these advanced analytics with just a click of a mouse or finger from any device (mobile or computer) with an internet connection.

Role Based Feature

Admin and the users (agent/branch managers) will have access to different features and portions of data based on their user credentials. There will also be a role of 'Superadmin' who will have the power to create or edit user credentials (username, password, expiry period, etc.).
Our System will also generate ‘Log’ files to keep the user-session information. Using that, an admin will be able to generate user statistics from the BI.

Dynamic Dashboard

The BI offers a dynamic interactive dashboard where users can narrow down to desired results by filtering and selecting various options. The customized reports and charts generated by the BI can be downloaded in pdf, CSV, Excel or jpeg format simply with a click.

Geo-location Map based Summary

A digital footprint of the branches/service points and transaction/sales summary is generated and updated automatically using high-quality leaflet maps. With the help of these attractive location-based summary maps, you will be able to see area-wise growths at a glance and make decisions on where you could spread your business.

Forecasting

Market Trend Analysis & Forecasting can be performed easily from the BI to foresee the growth of Transaction, IFR, Policy, Sales etc., for a certain period with the help of 8 Machine Learning Models together. The system empowers the decision-makers to utilize the power of intelligent forecasting to predict the future state of your business and make the decision based on that.

Performance Comparison

Performance comparison or identifying the top agents, business executives or branches are displayed inside the system. These performance charts are automatically updated on a daily/monthly basis based on the requirements.

Market Research Tool

Using the analytical power of the BI such as Clustering, Regression Analysis, Sentiment Analysis etc., a user will be able to identify its potential customers, understand market segmentation better and run targeted promotional activities based on that.
